You can change program settings from File->Customize
You can change the font and\or color of the polynomials settings
You can clear the log and history from File->Clear Log
The log is always saved before closing
You can prevent showing closing message from File->Customize
You can enter real or complex number to substitute in the polynomials such as: 5, 3+2i, 0+4i
You can view old operations by clicking on the list on the right of the window
